Program

Schedule of Programs

  • 9:00 AM
    Inaguration by Recitation of Quran kareem

    The event kicks off with the recitation of Quran Kareem, symbolizing the auspicious beginning of the program.

  • 9:10 AM
    Introduction to ICEST-2024 by General Chair

    The General Chair introduces the ICEST-2024 event, providing an overview and setting the tone for the day's activities.

  • 9:30 AM
    Talk of keynote Speaker for ICEST-2024

    A keynote speaker delivers a talk, sharing valuable insights and expertise related to the theme of ICEST-2024.

  • 10: 30 AM
    Felicitation of Keynote Speaker by ICEST-2024 Chair

    The chair of ICEST-2024 expresses gratitude and honors the keynote speaker for their contribution to the event.

  • 11:00 AM
    TEA BREAK

    Participants take a break to refresh themselves with a tea break, providing an opportunity for networking and informal discussions.

  • 11:15 AM
    Parallel Keynote Talks by all THEMES at respective Seminar halls

    Simultaneous keynote talks on different themes take place in various seminar halls, allowing participants to choose sessions of interest.

  • 12:30 PM
    LUNCH BREAK

    Participants break for lunch, providing a chance for networking and relaxation before the afternoon sessions.

  • 1:30 PM
    Parallel Session of paper Presentation for every THEMES in Seminar Halls

    Multiple sessions featuring paper presentations on different themes run concurrently in seminar halls, fostering in-depth discussions.

  • 03:30 PM
    TEA BREAK

    Another tea break provides participants with a brief interlude for networking and informal interactions.

  • 03:40 AM
    Validictory

    The event concludes with a valedictory session, summarizing the day's achievements and expressing gratitude to participants and contributors.
